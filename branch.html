NEW TECHNOLOGY

INTRODUCTION

Sustainable products and new technology are revolutionizing the way we live, work, and play. With advancements in renewable energy, energy efficient products, and sustainable materials, it's never been easier to reduce our environmental impact

From smart home devices to electric vehicles, there are more sustainable options than ever before. This presentation will explore the exciting possibilities of sustainable products and new technology, and how they can be used to create a more sustainable future.

SMART HOME

Smarthome technology is one of the most popular sustainable products on the market today. Smart thermostats, lighting, and appliances can all be used to reduce energy consumption and save money.

These products can be programmed to automatically turn off when not in use and can be monitored remotely. They provide convenience, comfort, and peace of mind, while also reducing our environmental impact.

ELECTRIC VEHICLES

Electric vehicles are becoming increasingly popular, as they are much more efficient than traditional gasoline- powered cars. These vehicles are powered by rechargeable batteries and produce zero emissions. Electric vehicles are becoming more affordable and offer a range of features and benefits. They are also much quieter than gasoline-powered cars, making them a great option for reducing noise pollution.

It’s not only individual vehicle owners who have better access to electric vehicles (EVs) than ever before—there are 160 electric and hybrid vehicle models available today—but municipalities are taking notice as well. In China, 300,000 electric buses hum down city streets every day. Their widespread adoption in China—an economic coup as much as a policy one—will entice European cities to follow suit. Although these eBuses have higher acquisition prices due to upfront battery costs, their total cost of ownership (TCO) is lower due to their independence from pricey diesel. They also eliminate local particulates, including SOx, NOx, and CO2, all major issues in most cities today.

PLASTIC RECYCLING

260 million tons of plastic waste is generated across the globe every year, but only 16 percent gets recycled. The plastics industry has the opportunity to move away from a “take, make, and dispose” business model and adopt a circular model, which aims to eliminate waste across sectors while creating economic, societal, and environmental benefits. One promising circular process is pyrolysis, which uses heat and the absence of oxygen to reconvert plastic waste back into liquid feedstock. The benefits are economic as much as environmental, with a recycling-based profit pool estimated at $55 billion by the next decade.

SOLAR POWER

Renewable energy continues to become cheaper and more accessible into 2019, a trend that has major implications for the nearly 1 billion people across the globe without access to electricity. While expanding the grid is part of the access solution, countries in sub-Saharan Africa and the Caribbean, which account for a majority of the world’s unelectrified population, are exploring renewable solutions like solar energy to bring energy quickly and inexpensively to millions. Innovative financing plans can help make previously unaffordable solar home systems (SHSs) a smart solution for communities that are too far from a reliable grid connection. A recent McKinsey assessment determined that SHSs can help power 150 million households by 2020.

WASTE

INTRODUCTION

Waste has a major impact on the environment, as it contributes to air and water pollution, land degradation, and global warming. It also takes up valuable space in landfills, and can be dangerous if not disposed of properly. Additionally, waste can have a negative impact on human health, as it can lead to the spread of disease.

WASTE MANAGEMENT

The use of sustainable products can have a significant impact on the amount of waste being produced. Sustainable products are designed to be reused, reducing the need for new products and the waste associated with them. Sustainable products can also help to reduce the amount of waste going to landfills, as they are made from biodegradable materials that can be broken down and recycled more easily. Solid waste management is a comprehensive discipline, including controlling the waste generation, storage, transfer, and disposal in the best way for public health. While the benefits of solid waste management have been realized all over the world, now it’s time for eco-friendly methods to swell. Using these techniques, we can save our precious environment in many ways! Let’s start with the three R’s rule one by one.

WASTE REDUCING & REUSING

Reducing waste is essential to preserving the environment, and there are several steps that can be taken to do so. These include reducing the use of single-use plastics, composting organic waste, and recycling whenever possible. Additionally, buying sustainable products can help reduce waste, as they are designed to last longer and produce less waste.

Reducing waste has numerous benefits, including reducing air and water pollution, conserving natural resources, and reducing greenhouse gas emissions. It also helps preserve landfills, and can improve human health by reducing the spread of disease. Additionally, reducing waste can have economic benefits, as it can help save money on waste disposal costs.
Definition of waste reduction is pretty simple, lessening or eliminating the number of materials initially used. Reusable serving utensils and trays instead of disposable items are perfect examples of waste reduction. Alternatively, reducing the amount you buy is the most essential of all the options to manage waste wisely. Why is waste reduction important? In fact, if each household mitigates its waste, the global garbage problem will be reduced as well.

BENIFITS OF RECYCLING

Recycling is crucial to lessen the carbon footprint and contribute toward a healthy environment and sustainability. As recycled products are used in the production of new products, the need for raw materials is eliminated.
For example, By recycling paper, fewer trees are cut down. While greenhouse gas amount is reduced, natural resources are conserved. These environmental facts explain the environmental benefits of recycling paper products, which help diminish deforestation and promote a balance in the environment.

ECOSYSTEM

INTRODUCTION

Sustainability is a critical aspect of the modern world. It is the way of ensuring that our environment and resources are used responsibly and efficiently. Sustainable product and ecosystem is a way of creating a system that is beneficial to both the environment and the people who use it. The sustainable product and ecosystem approach is focused on creating products and services that have a positive impact on the environment and society. This includes reducing waste and pollution, increasing energy efficiency, and creating products that are not harmful to the environment

WILDLIFE & FOREST

Sustainable products can help to protect wildlife by reducing the amount of pollution created by manufacturing processes. This can help to reduce acid rain, which can be toxic to some species. Sustainable products can also help to reduce the amount of water and energy used in production, which can help to conserve resources. By reducing the amount of pollution and conserving resources, sustainable products can have a positive impact on ecosystems and the wildlife that inhabit them.
The majority of the world’s biodiversity lives in, and depends on, forests.This is where it’s evolved over hundreds of thousands of years. Deforestation can wipe out huge swathes of this incredible richness within a matter of moments. According to the WWF’s recent Living Planet report, the current levels of deforestation make the species living in our rainforests some of the most endangered in the world.

POLLUTION & AIR QUALITY

Sustainable products are designed to be reused, recycled, or composted. This helps to reduce the amount of waste that is sent to land fills, and can also reduce the amount of pollution created by manufacturing processes. Sustainable products often require less energy to produce, resulting in fewer emissions. By reducing the amount of pollution created, sustainable products can have a positive effect on ecosystems. This can help to protect wildlife, reduce acid rain, and improve air quality.
Sustainable products can help to improve air quality by reducing the amount of pollution created by manufacturing processes. This can help to reduce acid rain, which can be toxic to some species. Sustainable products can also help to reduce the amount of water and energy used in production, which can help to conserve resources.By reducing the amount of pollution and conserving resources, sustainable products can have a positive impact on air quality and the ecosystems that  depend on it.

CONSERVING RESOURCES

Sustainable products are often designed to be energy efficient and require less water and energy to produce. This can help to conserve resources, which can be beneficial to ecosystems. By using fewer resources, sustainable products can help to reduce the amount of pollution created, which can help to protect wildlife. By conserving resources and reducing pollution, sustainable products can have a positive effect on ecosystems and the environment in general.

ECONOMY

INTRODUCTION

Sustainable products are becoming increasingly popular as an alternative to traditional products. They are made from renewable resources and are more environmentally friendly than their traditional counterparts. As more people become aware of the benefits of sustainable products, the demand for them increases, leading to an economic  impact.

POSITIVE & NEGATIVE IMPACT

Sustainable products can have a positive impact on the economy by creating jobs, increasing spending, and improving the environment. Sustainable products are often made from renewable resources, which require more labor and resources to produce than traditional products. This creates more jobs and increases spending in the local economy. Sustainable products also have a positive environmental impact, reducing pollution and conserving resources.
Many customers are beginning to realise the benefits of eco-friendly living. So, transforming your business from top to bottom can attract more clients. This will increase the revenue. 70-75% of customers within the age bracket of 15-20 years said they would pay more for products and services offered by environment-friendly companies. Some government firms and non-profit institutions also prefer giving contracts to companies that run in an eco-friendly manner. ESG funds are gaining more popularity. These funds support the companies that are going green.

Sustainable products can also have a negative impact on the economy. They are often more expensive than traditional products, which can make them unaffordable for some people. Additionally, the production of sustainable products can be more labor- intensive and require more resources, which can drive up the cost of  production.

LONG TERM BENIFITS

Despite the potential negative impacts, there are long- term benefits to using sustainable products. Sustainable products are often more durable and last longer than traditional products, which can reduce the need for replacements and save money in the long run. Additionally, sustainable products can help reduce pollution and conserve resources, which can benefit the environment.

GOVERNMENT SUPPORT

Governments can also play a role in promoting the use of sustainable products. Governments can provide incentives for businesses to use sustainable products and can create regulations to reduce the use of traditional products. These measures can help to reduce the negative impacts of sustainable products and encourage more people to use them.
Tax breaks are not the only government advantages offered to green businesses. There are a number of grants, subsidies and financing programs available as well for the company or entrepreneur who seeks to be eco-friendlier. The Environmental Protection Agency provides grants for qualified programs that are related to environmentally responsible approaches for a variety of business operations. The Small Business Administration (SBA) offers financing solutions to business organizations that support green solutions in new construction, retrofitting existing structures and the advancement of green technologies. These are but a few of the many government subsidies available to companies that effect environmentally friendly practices and solutions.
