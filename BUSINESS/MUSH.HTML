Mush is a premium brand committed to providing its customers with an exclusive range of bamboo textile products. The company was founded by Ayush Agarwal and Nihar Gosalia in 2018.


The brand sells eco-friendly and high-quality bamboo textile bath sets, bath towels, face towels and aims to replace cotton, polyester, and other similar fiber with bamboo fibers.


The offered range is designed using modern technology under the direction of experts.


Apart from this, these fabric and socks are known for their high softness, shrink resistance, easy usability, and long life.
